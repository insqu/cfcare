Foot Care

Some of the smallest bones in your body are in your feet, in fact there are 26 bones, 19 muscles and 40 joints in each foot.

When we consider that an average person walks one hundred thousand miles in a lifetime, it is no wonder they can become uncomfortable.

Common problems include callus (thickening of the skin), corns (heloma) or ingrowing toe nails (onychocryptosis) to name just a few.

If you are experiencing problems with your feet then we are here to help. We are a local, family business, and are flexible to meet your needs. Just give us a call on 07542601165, or send us an email info@carrsfootcare.com and help will be on its way.
